About Gyftr

A product of Knowledge.

  • 2016

    EECS 371 Knowledge Representation and Reasoning

    Taught by Professor Ken Forbus at Northwestern University, this class introduced the concept of KR&R. Our lessons inspired our group of 4 undergraduates to build Gyftr! We first thought of the idea based off all of our own personal struggles finding gifts for our friends and family. We hope our Gyftr application can be a building block for future KR&R based systems to build on.
